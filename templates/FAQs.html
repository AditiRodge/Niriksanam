FAQs - Nirikṣaṇam
1. What is Nirikṣaṇam?
Nirikṣaṇam is an AI-powered plagiarism detection tool designed to help students, educators, and institutions ensure academic work is original and free from plagiarism.
2. How does Nirikṣaṇam work?
Our AI algorithms scan your documents for potential matches with existing content across various sources. The tool then generates a detailed report showing where similarities may exist, helping you maintain academic integrity.
3. How do I use Nirikṣaṇam?
Using Nirikṣaṇam is easy! Just follow these steps:
  • Upload Your Document: Select the document you want to check for plagiarism.
  • Review Your Report: Once the scan is complete, view a detailed report showing any potential plagiarism and suggested improvements.
  • Download Your Report: After reviewing the report, you can download the final.
  • Make Necessary Changes: Based on the report, manually revise your document to improve originality before submitting or using it.
4. Do I need to create an account to use Nirikṣaṇam?
No, you don’t need to create an account to use Nirikṣaṇam. You can upload and check your documents without signing up.
5. Can Nirikṣaṇam detect all types of plagiarism?
While Nirikṣaṇam detects a wide range of plagiarism types, such as direct copying and paraphrasing, it’s always a good idea to also review your work for proper citations and references.
6. Can I use Nirikṣaṇam for free?
Yes, our platform is completely free to use. You can check your documents for plagiarism without any cost or limitations.
7. Is my data secure with Nirikṣaṇam?
Yes, we take your privacy seriously. All documents uploaded to Nirikṣaṇam are securely processed, and we do not store your personal information without your consent.
8. What file formats can I use with Nirikṣaṇam?
Nirikṣaṇam accepts a variety of file formats for plagiarism detection, including:
  • Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx)
  • PDF (.pdf)
  • Text Files (.txt)
Additionally, direct text can also be copied and pasted into the platform for plagiarism scanning.
